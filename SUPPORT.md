# Support

XSI-AIMS is a community-supported open specification. Help is provided on a best-effort basis by the steward team and the wider community — there is no paid support tier and no service-level agreement. This document points you to the right channel for what you need.

## Before you ask

Please search first — your question may already be answered:

- the specification text under [`spec/`](spec/) and the glossary index;
- existing [Issues](../../issues) (open and closed);
- existing [Discussions](../../discussions).

## Where to go

| You want to… | Use |
| --- | --- |
| Report a bug, contradiction, unclear definition, or ambiguity in the spec | **[Issues](../../issues)** |
| Ask a "how should I think about this?" or design question | **[Discussions](../../discussions)** |
| Propose a substantive change to normative behavior | The RFC process — see [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md) |
| Report a security-relevant ambiguity or unsafe requirement | **Email security@xtendedsystems.com** — see [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md). Do not open a public Issue. |
| Anything that doesn't fit the above | **Email aims@xtendedsystems.com** |

### Issues — bugs and ambiguities

Open an [Issue](../../issues) when you find something concrete and actionable: a contradiction between two sections, a definition that's unclear, a specification surface you can't implement as written, or an ambiguity that two careful readers could interpret differently. Cite the section number and quote the passage so the report is reproducible.

### Discussions — questions and design

Use [Discussions](../../discussions) for open-ended conversation: how a pattern is intended to be applied, how AIMS relates to a body of work you're using, design trade-offs you're weighing, or feedback on the multi-year roadmap. This is where the community gathers; most "how do I think about this?" threads belong here rather than in Issues.

### Email — everything else

For matters that don't fit Issues or Discussions — governance questions, patent-posture questions, press, partnership inquiries — reach the steward team at **aims@xtendedsystems.com**. Use a clear subject-line prefix (for example, `Governance` or `Patent question`) so the message is routed appropriately.

## What to expect

Issues and Discussions are monitored on a best-effort basis. Triage and response times vary; this is community-supported work from a small steward team, not a commercial support offering. There is no guaranteed response window for general support (security reports have a stated acknowledgement window — see [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md)).

## Code of Conduct

All support channels are governed by the project's [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Please keep interactions constructive and respectful.
